How to get there

The ceremony and celebration will be held at The Q Station, Manly

The Q Station entrance is easily accessible by car or ferry. A shuttle service is available on site to assist guests to navigate from the entrance to the ceremony and reception venues and back to the carpark after the event.

Car

If you are travelling by car, there is a car park at the entrance of Q station you can park in. From the entrance, you can take the complimentary shuttle service to the ceremony location by the front office.

Ferry

If you'd like to take public transport, consider the scenic Manly Ferry. It is a 35-minute walk from Manly or a 5-minute Uber/taxi trip. There may be carpool available to get guests from the ferry to the venue - please reach out to Colleen and Elliot for more information. Alternatively, there is a public bus or a Q Station shuttle that you can take to and from Manly Wharf.
